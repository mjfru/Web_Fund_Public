Why Indentation?

This page will cover two important concepts, proper indentation and parent-child-sibling relationship.

We, developers, don't indent our code to reflect the content. We indent to via the HTML tag's relationship to other tags. To center a paragraph title and indent at the start, we'll use CSS to do that.

On this page in particular, the h1 tag 'Why Indentation?' is a child of the body tag. It's on the same line of indentation, making it a sibling of the < p > tag.

So, is it really required to indent HTML?

It is, in fact, not required but highly, highly recommended.

Some of those reasons include:

  • Beautiful code can make you happy!
  • Less time to troubleshoot! Easier to spot missing closing tags because of indentation.
  • Much easier to browse through your own code, you'll know exactly where everything is and where it should belong.
  • Creates good coding habits that are transferable to other languages.
